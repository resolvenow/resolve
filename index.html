FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common concerns about loan resolution and support

Which types of loans does Resolve Now assist with?

We provide assistance for various kinds of loans, including personal loans, credit card dues, business loans, and other unsecured borrowings, depending on individual circumstances.

How long does the loan resolution process usually take?

The timeline varies based on the lender, loan amount, and case complexity, but most resolutions are achieved within a few weeks to a few months.

Will interest or penalty charges continue during the settlement process?

In many cases, we work to minimize additional interest and late fees by engaging with lenders and negotiating fair terms on your behalf.

How should I respond to calls from banks or recovery agents?

Once you are connected with us, we guide you on handling communication professionally and, where possible, act as an intermediary to reduce unnecessary pressure.

What if a lender initiates legal proceedings against me?

If legal action is initiated, our team helps you understand the situation and connects you with appropriate legal guidance to ensure your rights are protected.
