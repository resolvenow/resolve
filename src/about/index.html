Our Story

We Started Because the System Wasn't Fair

Financial difficulty can happen to anyone. A job loss, a medical emergency, a business that didn't survive — life doesn't warn you before it disrupts your finances. What follows can be brutal: mounting interest, relentless collection calls, threatening messages, legal notices at your door.

Most people facing this have no idea what their rights are, what options exist, or who they can trust. Banks have entire legal teams. Recovery agents are trained to apply pressure. The average borrower is alone.

We started Resolve Now to change that. We are a team of financial counsellors, legal advisors, and negotiation specialists based in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh — and we negotiate with lenders on your behalf. Legally. Transparently. Without false promises.
